Monica Lewis

Sediment

I am
Sentimental but the
Sediment is
Breaking me
And the ocean kisses the
Bonded tides
A billion splintering bits of what was
Once concrete
Now bits of heart stars
Sparkling
Still she knows when to pull
Back
And when to receive
I am
Still learning
Agasp and astonished
Sobbing
At her
Shores.
